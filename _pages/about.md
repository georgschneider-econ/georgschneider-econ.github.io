I am a Ph.D. candidate in Economics at the **University of Bonn** (Bonn Graduate School of Economics), and I recently visited **Yale University** as a visiting Ph.D. student. I am on the economics job market in **2026–2027**.

My research is in **financial economics, labor economics, and public economics**, with a focus on applied microeconomic and experimental methods. I study how labor markets interact with financial decision-making, the real effects of taxation on trade, and the determinants of team performance.

My **job market paper**, *Bankers' Outside Options and Financial Risk* (with Valentin Kecht), shows that bankers with better labor market outside options take more financial risk, pointing to a labor mobility channel through which labor market conditions amplify credit cycles.

You can read more on my [research page](/publications/) and download my [CV](/files/CV_Georg_Schneider.pdf).
